# Troubleshooting & FAQ

Common issues and how to resolve them.

!!! info "This page is being written"
    The outline below is what it will cover. For anything not here, see [Support](support.md).

## What this page will cover

- **Controller not found / won't connect** — USB, the WinUSB driver (Zadig), and **Refresh**. See
  [Installation & driver](getting-started/installation.md).
- **"Device not configured" / Run is blocked** — import a `markcfg7` via the
  [Device Setup Wizard](getting-started/first-run.md).
- **Marks come out rotated or mirrored** — galvo X/Y assignment and mirror toggles in
  [Hardware & Device Setup](hardware-setup.md).
- **Distortion / shapes not round** — lens correction and `.cor` files, in
  [Lenses, Corrections & Calibration](lenses-corrections.md).
- **Marks look weak or out of focus** — power/speed/frequency and the lens **focal Z height**.
- **Licensing problems** — activation and renewal, in [Updates & Licensing](updates-licensing.md).
- **Reading the log** — where the app log lives and what to send with a support request
  (see [Reference](reference.md)).
- **Movement behaves unexpectedly** — the open-loop jog model (jogs queue; no per-move confirmation) — see
  [Jog, Homing & Terminal](jog-terminal.md).
