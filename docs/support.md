## Before you reach out

A quick check of [Troubleshooting & FAQ](troubleshooting.md) resolves most common issues
(controller not found, driver setup, correction/`.cor` questions, licensing).

When emailing support, it helps to include:

- Your **FocuZ version** (see **About**).
- Your **controller model** and lens.
- What you did, what you expected, and what happened.
- If relevant, the app **log** (see [Reference](reference.md) for its location).
