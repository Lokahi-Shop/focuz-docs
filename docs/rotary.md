# Rotary Marking

Mark cylindrical parts by driving a rotary axis between marking passes. Configure it under **Device ▸
Rotary Setup**.

!!! info "This page is being written"
    The outline below is what it will cover.

## What this page will cover

- **Rotary setup** — enable, **Chuck vs. Roller** mode, rotary **axis** (X or Y), and part **diameter**.
- **Split & overlap** — marking around a part larger than one field by splitting into rotation steps with
  overlap.
- **Motor settings** — steps per rotation, min/max speed, acceleration, and return speed.
- **BJJCZ rotary controls** — offset, jog (CW/CCW), Go Home, Set Zero, and Test Move.
- **Workflow & caveats** — return-to-zero behavior, and interaction with lens correction.
